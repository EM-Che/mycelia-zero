Mycelia Zero is version 0 of the decentralized Mycelia software.

It is in part a rewrite of Labitrack v2, and in part a precursor to Mycelia. It is the bridge between Labitrack and Mycelia.

The goal is to switch Labitrack over to the core technologies of Mycelia (CouchDB, node.js and ElasticSearch), while adding only a few features to the Labitrack software (primarily simple access level differentation and centralized aggregation).
